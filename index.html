the industries where we add our expertise

 

Banking

From AML & KYC to reward schemes or reaching the unbanked, our technology and training can help identify and blockchain-enable processes and services.

Healthcare

Complete and immutable family histories of every related diagnosis and condition - all derived from a single master key and cryptographically secured by patient and staff.

Accounting

Create programmable processes and automated auditing trails that increase transparency and effeciency while lowering costs and removing the room for error.

Legal

On the blockchain code is law - our technology can help with document notarization, copyright protection, and dispute settlement via third party key management.

Governance

Distributed consensus technology can help increase the accountability of public officials and offer total transparency in voting and elections.

Logistics

Keep track of your shipping and provinence cycles with autonomous systems that can verify and record every element of your logistical processes.

Gaming
